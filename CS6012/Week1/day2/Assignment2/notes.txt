* Books:
    - represented by ISBN, author, title -> fixed once book created (static final)
* Library book - book coupled with holder (person that checked out the book) and due date
    - attributes change when book is checked in and out of the library
    - Assume holders are unique
    - Holders can be representated with a name, phonenumber, or email address (same field)
        - Should allow any type for holder to anticipate future types
* Operations
    - Retrieve list of all books in the library sorted by ISBN or author
    - Retrieve list of overdue library books
